Problem Definition

  • There may have lots of food in our refrigerator.
  • It would be fun if the refrigerator can tell us what we can cook via these ingredients inside!
  • Sometimes, we will put food until passing its expiration date which causes much waste. If the refrigerator can "remind" us or send the message to our phones, it would reducing wasted food at home.
  • Moreover, it can adjust the electricity which controls temperature by detecting the amount of food.

What We Want to Do >> Please Refrigerator

An application that make us have better user experienxe when using refrigerator.

Target population
  • People who use refrigerator.
Why is it important
  • Reduce wasted food.
  • Add more surprises and convenient in life.
Who are the stakeholders
  • Refrigerator Company
  • Food Company
